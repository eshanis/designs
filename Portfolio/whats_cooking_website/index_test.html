Hello and welcome! I am happy you are here. Finding the right balance in life is an edventure - education and adventure. I love to paint, cook and bake with my children. I also love to experiment with food and obsess over making it nutritious, healthy and yet delicious. I read a lot about nutrition and health and try various diets on me ane my family to see how it actually works. I have been a vegetarian all my life. People always ask me if it is difficult and what I eat? I am constantly learning from my experiences and travels. Experiencing different cultures, traditions and food is what I love. The idea for this website came along because I kept asking my mom for recipes of food I grew up eating and wanted my children to try. The website has traditional indian recipes and lots of fun and healthy recipes that I have enjoyed cooking and my family and friends have enjoyed eating.
