About Me

My name is Eshani. For me finding the right balance in life is an "edventure" - education and adventure.

I love to learn new things, travel and experience different cultures, food. Throw in some Hi tech in there and it's a perfect blend.


Every decade has brought new adventures and new experiences for me. My love for learning new things and ability to adapt to the changes has lead me to my current position at HCL-Tech. I have a degree in Architecture, went back to school for a degree in Early Childhood Education and then a Certificate in Web Development.


I have worn many hats in the past. Architect, Preshool/Kindergarten teacher, Yoga Instructor, daughter, wife, mother. I love to paint, cook and bake with my children. I also love to experiment with food and obsess over making it nutritious, healthy and yet delicious. Yoga, dance, and fitness is another passion of mine. As a family, we love to travel and experience different cultures.

My goal is to work in an environment that gives me the opportunity to use my new skills.
